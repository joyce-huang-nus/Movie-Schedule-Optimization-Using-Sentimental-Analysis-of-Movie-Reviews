# Movie-Schedule-Optimization-Using-Sentimental-Analysis-of-Movie-Reviews

Therefore, film arrangement is a significantly essential task for cinemas. Traditionally, theaters would predict a high box office for a movie with famous producers, popular cast and high budget and allocate more screenings to these kinds of movies. This is also how audiences made their decision in the past. However, with the spread of SNS and online critics commentaries, people tend to search for comments on the Internet before buying a ticket.
